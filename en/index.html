    EFFICIENT DOCUMENT AUTOMATION

    Many applications struggle with document creation:
    Rigid layouts, hard-to-maintain templates, fragile generation logic – and every small change needs a developer.

    We simplify that by integrating TX Text Control directly into your existing .NET software – whether it’s WinForms, WPF, Blazor, or ASP.NET. This enables your users to create documents directly within the system – automatically filled, editable, and tailored to the current context. It reduces complexity, speeds up processes, takes pressure off your developers – and turns your software into a true document hub.

    cog

    In-app document creation

    Users create documents right inside your application – auto-filled, based on editable templates, with no external tooling required.

    lightbulb

    Templates your teams can maintain

    Business departments can maintain and update templates themselves – no developer needed. That saves capacity and accelerates change.

    processor

    Document workflows with e-signatures

    Documents are generated exactly where they’re needed – in sales, customer communication, or internal workflows.
    With TX Text Control, we implement complete document flows directly inside your app: from creation and review to digital signing – secure and industry-ready, for example in healthcare, finance, or the public sector.

    award

    Modernization included

    As a TX Text Control Partner, we can also modernize your .NET-based solution – WinForms, WPF, or Web – and integrate document automation seamlessly into your architecture.
    We also support the new Linux version of TX Text Control – ideal for cloud-native or containerized SaaS scenarios.
