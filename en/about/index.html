About Us

Welcome to Quality Bytes GmbH, a hub of software development expertise and innovation. Our dedicated team of software developers is passionate about creating high-quality, cutting-edge software solutions designed to empower businesses of all sizes. Our mission is to empower software companies by delivering superior document automation solutions and mobile apps, enhancing their offerings and reducing time-to-market. We specialize in mobile app development and document automation, helping our clients enhance their offerings and accelerate their digital transformation journey

Founded in the summer of 2018 in Sinzig, Germany, Quality Bytes began as a small venture with a big vision. Led by Microsoft MVP André Krämer, we set out to deliver premium, customized software solutions to clients of all sizes and industries. Today, our focus is on software companies with existing industry solutions and internal IT departments of large enterprises, providing them with solutions for document automation and business mobile apps. Our team initially consisted of André, his wife supporting us in the back office, two trainees, and an intern. Now, we are a dynamic team of 19 members, including 15 highly skilled software engineers.

As our team grew, so did our need for space. In December 2020, we moved to a larger office in Bad Breisig, Germany. Today, while our headquarters remains in Bad Breisig, we’ve embraced the flexibility of remote work, with some team members opting to work from home. After five years of working exclusively for German clients, we are proud to serve our first international clients since 2023.

At Quality Bytes,wWe are committed to an agile approach to software development and most of our developers are certified Professional Scrum Developers (PSD). We also have certified Scrum Masters (PSM) and Product Owners (PSPO) on board to ensure that our team works efficiently and effectively while maintaining the flexibility to adapt to changing requirements.

Our technical proficiency in TX Text Control and .NET MAUI, coupled with in-depth knowledge in .NET, ASP.NET, Azure, Blazor, and Angular, empowers us to deliver superior software solutions tailored to the complex needs of our clients. From our headquarters in Bad Breisig, Germany, and various home office locations throughout the country, we successfully cater to clients worldwide.

At Quality Bytes, we foster an environment of continuous learning, innovation, and growth. Our team members are always honing their skills, staying on the cutting edge of technology, and contributing to a culture that values quality, teamwork, and customer success above all.

Our values

Carefulness and quality awareness

Software is not "hacked together" at our company. It does not mature at the customer but is developed, tested, and documented by us with the greatest care and precision. Each of our team members always works to the highest quality standards to ensure the long-term success of our customer solutions.

We are partners

We are not some software company writing source code lines for the sake of it. We are partners of our customers and pursue a common goal.

We deliver results

We take our promises seriously and deliver world-class software. Our customers can rely on us not just to deliver promises, but to deliver great software.

Clear communication

We don't talk to our customers in IT jargon but in their language. Only clear and understandable communication enables us to create great software that delights our customers.

Innovative and always on the pulse of time

To be able to deliver innovative work results for our customers, we constantly educate ourselves. By promoting continuous learning, we not only ensure the high quality of our work results but at the same time contribute to the personal satisfaction of our employees.

We are a team

Software is not created at Quality Bytes GmbH by lonely individuals. We value a pleasant working environment and pursue together the goal of achieving the best result for our customers. We respect that everyone has their strengths and weaknesses, recognize successes, and thus help each other to grow.

We have fun with what we do

Only those who really enjoy doing something can consistently produce great work results. Therefore, we create a working atmosphere in which everyone feels comfortable, where people enjoy their work and like to go to work. Our goal is that the team members of Quality Bytes GmbH perceive their work as personal enrichment and not as a duty.
