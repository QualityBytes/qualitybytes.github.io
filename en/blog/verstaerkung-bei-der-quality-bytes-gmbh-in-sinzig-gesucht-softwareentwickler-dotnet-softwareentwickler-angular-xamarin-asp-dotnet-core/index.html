Reinforcement as Software Developer .NET, Frontend Developer Angular, Xamarin Developer or ASP.NET Core Developer wanted

  • André Krämer
  • 19.03.2019

This might be your new desk

For our team in Sinzig at the Rhine, between Bonn and Koblenz, we are looking for several software developers with immediate effect. We develop exciting customer solutions in the web and mobile environment in a team of four developers.

In doing so, we rely on modern technologies, tools, and approaches, such as

  • ASP.NET Core
  • Angular
  • Xamarin
  • Azure DevOps
  • git
  • Unit Testing
  • Typescript
  • C#

We currently have various positions available. Among others, we are looking for three .NET developers who can either develop ASP.NET Core or Xamarin or both, and a frontend developer with a focus on Angular.

So if you feel like working on innovative projects in a familiar team with flat hierarchies, regular working hours, and good opportunities for further development, then take a look at our vacancies and apply.

About the author

André Krämer

Dipl.-Inform. (FH) André Krämer, Msc. is a co-founder and the CEO of Quality Bytes GmbH. The multi-awarded Microsoft MVP is working in the field of software development since 1997 and has specialized in the development with Microsoft .NET in 2002.

His main emphasizes are Xamarin, ASP.NET, Angular, Azure DevOps, TX Text Control, and the analysis of memory leaks and performance issues.

He is a regular author for linkedIn Learning, where he already published more than 25 German video courses.
