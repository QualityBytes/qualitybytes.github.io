    Migrate from Xamarin.Forms to .NET MAUI

    Xamarin.Forms reached end of life in May 2024—now it’s time to move forward.

    Without migrating, your app will soon be unmaintainable and stuck in limbo.Google and Apple will reject updates for Xamarin.Forms apps, and the longer you wait, the greater the risk of security issues and incompatibility. Migrating to .NET MAUI keeps your app running—and unlocks better performance, a modern user experience, and long-term viability.

    This is a pivotal moment. Don’t risk it with trial and error. Trust proven experts: we’ve successfully migrated multiple apps and actively contribute to the .NET MAUI framework—bringing deep expertise and direct insight from Microsoft’s product team.

    WHY MIGRATING TO .NET MAUI MATTERS NOW

    Migrating from Xamarin.Forms to .NET MAUI isn’t just a routine update—it’s your opportunity to secure the future of your app. .NET MAUI brings faster performance, a modern user experience, and an architecture designed for long-term scalability and maintainability.

    But be warned: migration has its pitfalls, especially for complex apps or highly customized codebases. Deep framework knowledge is key to avoiding costly setbacks.

    Grab our whitepaper to discover what really counts—or cut straight to the solution and bring in our expert team to ensure a smooth, stress-free migration.
