Why you should work with us

Innovativ projects

For a software developer, there is little more frustrating than working with outdated technologies. Ultimately, the productive use of current tools and programming libraries is an investment in your own professional future. To ensure that none of our employees end up on the technological sidelines, we pay attention the use of modern technologies in our projects.

Clear working hours

Many software companies expect regular overtime from their employees. Not us! With us, you work 40 hours per week, no more and no less. If it should exceptionally become more, then we provide timely time compensation.

Our developers sleep at home

Being on the road a lot professionally is exciting. At least in the beginning. At some point, it just becomes a burden on your private life. That's why we carry out our projects at our office and not at the customer's site. So you can sleep in your own bed at home and not in a different hotel every week.

We support and value continuous learning

In software development, nothing is worse than stagnation. Therefore, we attach great importance to the targeted training of our employees. In addition to internal training courses, we also make it possible for them to attend specialist conferences. Furthermore, we cooperate with a local University of Applied Sciences and offer the possibility of dual studies.

We are in your area

Do you have to drive an hour or more into the city to work every morning and suffer through rush hour traffic? Not with us!

We work together, not against each other

A good working atmosphere is vital to us. The only rivalry we have is when we play a game together on our Xbox during the lunch break.
