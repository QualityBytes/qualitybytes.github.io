Document Generation

Customized software solutions for automated creation of business documents

  1. Home
  2.   /   Software
  3.   /   Document Generation

Every day, countless documents are created in offices. Contracts, invoices, quotations, status reports, vacation requests, travel requests, labels, attendance certificates, or name badges are just a few examples.

Most such documents are probably created manually. So, an employee copies an old file, replaces (hopefully) all variable components of the document with the new values, saves the file, possibly exports it as a PDF, and prints it.

It is obvious that this process is not only very time-consuming, but above all error-prone.

In practice, the following problems often occur:

  • The employee forgets to replace some variable parts of the document with current values, so that, for example, there is an incorrect amount on the invoice or an incorrect contact person in the contract.
  • An old template with outdated content is used accidentally.
  • For source data that needs to be copied from multiple target systems, an incorrect record is opened so that the pasted records in the document do not match.
  • Variable document parts are typed, which can cause typing errors.

To avoid these errors, Quality Bytes GmbH offers the development of individual software solutions for automated document creation. By connecting your in-house software systems, such as Microsoft SharePoint, SAP, Microsoft Dynamics NAV or, if required, direct access to your internal databases, our software solutions enable the time-saving creation of correct documents based on your current company data.

Our modern template editor, which is reminiscent of Microsoft Word, runs as a browser-based HTML5 application in the Inter- and Intranet, as well as on the Windows desktop. Of course, our document engine also runs in the Inter- or Intranet, as well as a desktop application on Windows. On request, we are also happy to integrate mobile devices such as smartphones or tablets into the document automation process.

Technically, we work in this environment with the tools of the company Text Control. André Krämer, co-founder and CEO of Quality Bytes GmbH has been a selected implementation partner of Text Control GmbH for many years. He is also one of the few certified Text Control trainers and consultants worldwide and is in close contact with the development team of Text Control GmbH.

Based on this strategic cooperation, we can develop outstanding document automation systems that do not only enormously increase the quality of your finished documents, but also significantly reduce the time and effort required to produce the finished document. The time saved will not only save you money, but will also enable your clerks to concentrate on more important tasks.
