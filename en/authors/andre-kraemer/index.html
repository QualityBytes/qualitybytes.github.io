André Krämer

Beschreibung

Dipl.-Inform. (FH) André Krämer, Msc. is a co-founder and the CEO of Quality Bytes GmbH. The multi-awarded Microsoft MVP is working in the field of software development since 1997 and has specialized in the development with Microsoft .NET in 2002.

His main emphasizes are Xamarin, ASP.NET, Angular, Azure DevOps, TX Text Control, and the analysis of memory leaks and performance issues.

He is a regular author for linkedIn Learning, where he already published more than 25 German video courses.
