    How to find us

    Our office is picturesquely located with a view of the Rhine at the southern exit of Bad Breisig, just a stone’s throw from the train station. The second parking lot on the right under the building is reserved for our visitors and marked with a “Quality Bytes” sign. If you are arriving by public transport, we are easily accessible by both bus and train. From the “Bad Breisig - Bahnhof” stop, you can reach us after a short, five-minute walk.
